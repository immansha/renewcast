GUJARAT STATE LOAD DESPATCH CENTRE
OPERATIONAL PROTOCOL FOR SOLAR GENERATORS
Protocol: GSLDC/SOL/2024/07

SECTION 1 - SCHEDULING

1.1 Day-Ahead Submission:
Submit schedules by 09:00 IST for next day in 15-minute blocks (96 slots).
Format: GSLDC portal or XML via SFTP.

1.2 Intra-Day Revision:
Revisions allowed up to 2 hours before affected block.
Maximum revision: plus or minus 20% of original schedule.

SECTION 2 - COMMUNICATION

Normal Operations:
GSLDC Control Room: 079-23256000 (24x7)
SCADA data: mandatory, 5-second resolution

Abnormal Events (deviation above 15%):
Immediate phone call to control room.
Email within 30 minutes: dispatch@gsldc.gov.in
Written report Form GSLDC-11 within 24 hours.

SECTION 3 - MAINTENANCE NOTIFICATION

Planned: 72 hours advance notice via coordination portal.
Forced Outage: notify within 15 minutes, estimated restoration within 1 hour.

SECTION 4 - PERFORMANCE AND PENALTIES

4.1 Availability Target:
Annual: 97%. Peak demand (16:00-22:00): 99% if solar resource is available.

4.2 Forecasting Accuracy:
Monthly MAPE must be below 15%.
Three consecutive months above 15% triggers performance review.

4.3 DSM:
CERC DSM regulations apply. See CERC Merit Order 2025 Section 3.

SECTION 5 - SPECIAL PROTOCOLS

5.1 Cyclone Protocol:
When IMD issues cyclone warning for Gujarat coast:
  Pre-emptively schedule zero generation from coastal plants.
  Notify GSLDC 6 hours before expected impact.

5.2 Grid Frequency Emergency:
If frequency drops below 49.5 Hz:
  All solar generators must immediately maximise output.
  Override all voluntary curtailment agreements.
